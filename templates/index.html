Sally's Gardening Store Database Management

This webpage is designed for employees to manage database entries. For all database tables: new entries can be added, and existing entries can be searched, edited, or deleted. To view or manage a table, click on the name of the table you wish to view on the top banner.

To add an entry to a table, enter the information in the given form, and click the 'Add' button.

To search for entries in a table, enter the information to be searched in the form and click the 'Search' button. You can search for entries matching any amount of columns as desired.

To update an entry, navigate to the entry in the table, click 'Update', and change the desired information in the form, and click the 'Submit' button.

To delete an entry in a table, navigate to the entry in the table, and click the 'Delete' button.
